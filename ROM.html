Sarah is a designer forming and creating our digital rights

Winning many awards including last yearâ€™s NESTA New Radical, Forbes 30 under 30 and a Creative Conscience Gold prize, she founded IF, a design studio where she works as Director. IF is working with a variety of clients including DeepMind, Consumers International and the Co-op. Previously Sarah co-founded WikiHouse Foundation, a non-profit organisation.

Sarah sits on the board for Tech for Good and as a practitioner for the Research Institute in the Science of Cyber Security.

She has expertise in technology, data and networks in the public domain. Sarah recently spoke to a Parliamentary Select Committee, expressing her views on data sharing and accountability of machine learning.

Sarah is a member of the Robin Hood Co-operative, an activist hedge fund that invests in projects that protect and promote the commons.
