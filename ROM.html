Sarah founded IF to work on ambitious projects involving interaction, data, and networks in the public domain. A NESTA New Radical awardee, Sarah sits on the practitioner panel for the Research Institute in the Science of Cyber Security. Sarah regularly gives talks about privacy, security and design.

Sarah is a member of the Robin Hood Co-operative, an activist hedge fund that invests in projects that protect and promote the commons.
