Planetary Scale Design

An ongoing research project exploring, anticipating and debating what planetary scale systems mean for our future, in collaboration with Ben Vickers, Ola Moller and Carina Niamh.

Alternet

The Alternet is a proposal for a telecommunications network. It is imagined as a public utility for the Internet age - created, controlled and owned by everyone. The network was positioned between the data sharing capacity of the the Internet and the obfuscation of the Darknets. The Alternet gives people ownership and control of their data through a speculative technology called data licences.
